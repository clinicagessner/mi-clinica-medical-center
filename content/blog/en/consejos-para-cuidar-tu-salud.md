## Take Care of Your Health with These 5 Practical Tips

At Clínica Hispana Nueva Salud Gessner we know that prevention is the best medicine. That's why we want to share five essential tips to keep your family healthy.

### 1. Don't Ignore Your Symptoms

We often think a constant headache, excessive tiredness or a cough that won't go away are minor things. However, these can be signs that something else is going on in your body.

**See a doctor if you experience:**
- Fever lasting more than 3 days
- Persistent pain anywhere in the body
- Extreme tiredness with no apparent reason
- Sudden weight changes

### 2. Get Regular Medical Checkups

Don't wait until you feel sick to see a doctor. Preventive checkups can detect health problems before they become something serious.

**We recommend:**
- A general checkup once a year
- Blood pressure check every 6 months
- Annual blood tests (glucose, cholesterol)
- Specific exams based on your age and gender

### 3. Manage Your Chronic Conditions

If you have diabetes, high blood pressure or high cholesterol, consistent follow-up is essential. At our Hispanic clinic we offer chronic condition management programs with personalized care in Spanish.

### 4. Keep Your Vaccines Up to Date

Vaccines aren't just for children. Adults also need protection against the flu, tetanus and other diseases. Ask at your next visit which vaccines you need.

### 5. Take Care of Your Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Stress, anxiety and depression can affect your overall well-being. Don't hesitate to talk to your doctor if you feel overwhelmed.
